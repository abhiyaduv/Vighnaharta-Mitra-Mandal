Our Story of 28 Years

From a handful of devoted friends to a mandal that unites an entire community — this is our journey of faith, service and celebration.

History

Founded in 1998 by a small group of neighbourhood friends, Vighnaharta Mitra Mandal began as a modest Ganesh Utsav celebration. Over 28 years it has grown into one of the most awaited festivals in the area, blending tradition with grand artistry.

Mission

To celebrate Ganeshotsav with devotion and discipline, while using the festival spirit to serve society through health camps, tree plantation drives and community welfare initiatives.

Vision

To be a mandal that the next generation is proud to inherit — rooted in culture, open to innovation, and always in service of the community that sustains it.

Achievements

Recognised for eco-friendly Ganesh idols, bUityfull decoration themes, and consistent civic contributions including health camps and neighbourhood welfare drives.

Our Journey

1998

The Beginning

A group of 12 friends installed the first small Ganesh idol in the lane.

2005

Community Grows

The mandal registered officially and began organising cultural competitions for children.

2012

Seva Begins

First free health check-up camp organised alongside the festival, now an annual tradition.

2018

Eco-Friendly Pledge

Mandal transitioned fully to eco-friendly, Shadu-clay idols and immersion practices.

2026

28th Year

Celebrating our largest Utsav yet, with this website marking a new digital chapter.
